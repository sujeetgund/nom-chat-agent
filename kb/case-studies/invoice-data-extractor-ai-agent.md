# Invoice Data Extractor – Turn Scanned Bills into Structured Data with AI

*by Divam Digest*

---

Manual invoice processing is time-consuming, error-prone, and expensive. At DivamTech, we built the Invoice Data Extractor Agent to eliminate bottlenecks in AP/AR workflows and bring structure to unstructured data.

## 🧾 The Problem

- Invoices arrive in various formats: PDF scans, emails, screenshots
- Finance teams must manually extract dates, totals, taxes, and vendor info
- Manual entry leads to data entry delays and mistakes

## 🤖 The AI Agent Solution

Our Invoice Extractor AI agent:

- Accepts invoice uploads (PDF, PNG, etc.)
- Uses OCR and LLM parsing to extract key fields
- Outputs structured JSON data ready for integration

## ⚙️ System Blueprint

- **Interface:** Upload form
- **LLMs Used:** OpenAI GPT-4o + OCR layer
- **Output Format:** JSON (Vendor Name, Date, GST, Total, Line Items)
- **Time to Launch:** Easy

## 📈 Business Wins

- ⏱ Reduces processing time by 90%
- 💵 Increases accuracy of payable records
- 🔗 Integrates directly with ERP or finance dashboards
